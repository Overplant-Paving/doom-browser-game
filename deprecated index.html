DOOM in Your Browser

Experience the classic 1993 first-person shooter DOOM right in your browser! No downloads required.

Game Controls

  • Arrow keys or WASD: Move
  • Ctrl or Space: Fire
  • Alt: Strafe
  • Shift: Run
  • Enter: Use/Open
  • 1-9: Select weapons
  • Esc: Menu
